Biography

Longhui Yu is a MPhil student at Peking University, advised by Prof. Yuesheng Zhu. Currently, I am sincerely looking for a Ph.D. opportunity starting 2024 FALL. Here is my CV. I am very lucky to work with Dr. Weiyang Liu and Prof. Adrian Weller from University of Cambridge, Dr. Lanqing Hong from National University of Singapore, and Prof. Zhenguo Li from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

My research interest broadly lies in Machine Learning methods and applications. Previously, I spent wonderful time with my collaborators working on Large Language Model, AI Alignment, Scalable Learning(Continual Learning, Semi‑supervised Learning), Distribution Robust(Long‑tailed Learning, Out‑of‑Distribution Detection), Neural Collapse. Meanwhile, I also pay attention to the real-world applications of AI, such as Autonomous Driving, NeRF, and Vector Font Synthesis.

Currently, I am actively looking for a Ph.D. opportunity starting 2024 FALL! For Ph.D. Research, I am curious and passionate about any research topic that has the potential to hold immense value for human society. Please contact me if there is a good fit!
